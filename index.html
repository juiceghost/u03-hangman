Uppgift 03 - Hangman

Welcome to hangman!

When you click Start a new game, the computer will choose a random word.
At that point a number of boxes will appear. The number of boxes matches the number of letters in the word.
Your goal is to guess the word by clicking on the letter buttons.
For each correct guess a letter will appear in the correct box for the word.
